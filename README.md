# 🍲 Mapping Memory, Mapping Meals

**India's very first attempt to digitise indigenous communities' food culture, foodways & heritage**

A community digital humanities archive preserving the endangered food culture and culinary heritage of West Bengal's indigenous communities — Santal, Toto, Rajbangshi, Mech, Lodha, Rabha, Garo, and beyond.

🌐 **Live Website:** https://mappingmemorymappingmeals.github.io/mappingmemoryproject/

🗺 **Interactive Archive Map:** https://tinyurl.com/4ukxfv2n

---

## About the Project

Mapping Memory, Mapping Meals is India's first community digital humanities project dedicated exclusively to preserving the endangered food culture and culinary heritage of West Bengal's indigenous communities. The project was conceived and executed during a summer research internship at the **Department of Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS), Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala**.

### Key Statistics
- **97** geocoded food entries
- **7+** indigenous communities documented
- **10** districts covered across West Bengal
- **154** recipe videos in the Tribe Family Cooking dataset
- **Multi-layer** data architecture capturing everything from ecology to sacred offerings to deep historical origins

## Sustainability

This archive follows the [Endings Project](https://endings.uvic.ca/) five-component framework for long-term digital preservation: static-web architecture, flat open-standard data formats, version control, distributed archiving, and zero server-side dependencies.
